WHAT'S TRENDING

The dynamic business landscape and rapidly changing consumer needs are driving companies to join the 360-degree customer experience bandwagon to stay relevant and competitive. Intense competition and evolving consumer expectations are creating a pressing need for companies to attain clear insights into customer behavior, preferences, purchasing patterns and interactions for enhanced experience and edge in the digital world. Representing the focal point of businesses, the modern customer requires companies to be consistent and agile in service quality. The new customer has created the need for businesses to deliver consistent, tailored experience through all touch-points. The scenario highlights the significance of 360-degree customer experience that presents opportunities for companies to grow the business in a strategic manner.


Accelerated transition of organizations towards digital transformation for delivering enhanced value to customers entails the need for new paradigms and strategies. The modern landscape has created the requirement for businesses to deliver value through personalized experiences from different digital channels like social media, mobile apps and messaging apps. These channels need to be connected for offering the desired, tailored experience. Companies are facing rising pressure to push digitalization while delivering engaging customer experiences. With customer being one of the crucial ingredients for business success, digital customer experience has gained notable attention. The aspect holds particular relevance amid the rise of remote working, sales and service that requires companies to re-imagine the experience, which is moving from in-person towards digital. The scenario points towards the significance of all touch-points related to customer engagement. Focusing on the customer amid the expedited digitalization requires companies to consider smarter approaches for delivering automation and self-services in a seamless manner. The immense shift towards digital channels is also prompting businesses to find new ways for omnichannel engagement for customers. Blurring lines between sales and service in the contactless digital world require companies to act in a highly proactive manner. The landscape presents significant opportunities for 360-degree customer view to emerge as a crucial component, requiring companies to access and present all customer information in the relevant manner for better decisions and customer experience. The 360-degree customer view and digital personalized experience hold close associations and are facilitated through integration of APIs, data and applications. The holistic view of customer information allows companies to deliver personalized experiences, which represent indispensable element of the digital transformation.


  • Holistic Customer View - Getting the 360-degree customer view holds pivotal significance for businesses to thrive, push revenue generation, drive customer satisfaction and improve brand loyalty. The 360-degree approach covers an extensive data collection strategy all touch-points and channels used by customers while interacting or communicating with the company. The approach yields valuable data to help businesses in gaining holistic view of customers. The use of data, such as purchase history, demographics, communication, service requests and feedback helps companies identify customer preferences and trends to shape the customer experience.
  • Predictive Analysis - The availability of different types of data related to the customer in one place provides clear insights into customer behavior and paves way for better predictions regarding future needs. In addition, customer information such as accounts, purchases, interactions, preferences, service requests and social media mentions support approaches to predict customer preferences for personalized recommendations. The 360-degree customer view provides companies with the opportunity to offer the right product at the right time and cross-sell. The approach results in high customer satisfaction along with high chances of repeat purchases.
  • Better Loyalty & Collaboration - The 360-degree customer view allows companies to deliver enhanced experience to customers by offering deeper insights into their buying behavior, preference and future requirements. The approach helps businesses in offering personalized experiences on the basis of customer data obtained from different channels used by customers during interaction with the brand. Offering the desired product and service to customers plays an important role in boosting brand loyalty. The 360-degree view ensures enhanced collaboration among service, sales and marketing teams that can share customer information in a seamless manner for better decisions. The collaboration can push revenues and help companies in retaining more customers.
  • Personalized Experiences - Holistic insights into customer preferences and behavior allow companies to make informed decisions and deliver personalized services in line with specific requirements of customer. The 360-degree customer view coupled with information such as patterns, preferred communication channel, preferences and purchase history enables businesses to come up with effective and personalized recommendations. The approach also opens opportunities regarding cross-selling and up-selling products and services. The entire strategy improves customer satisfaction and experience, leading to enhanced profits and customer retention.

The 360-degree customer view is set to dramatically transform customer experience in the future and gain immense traction as the modern customer expects organizations to predict their needs, solve problems fast and personalize the communication. The changing business landscape needs delivery of enhanced value through tailored customer experiences from different digital channels along with physical delivery. The 360-degree customer view holds relevance as an increasing number of customers are ready to pay more for enhanced customer experience. Customers are highly unlikely to return to brands following a single incident of bad experience. The customer experience is anticipated to continue remaining the key priority for organizations in the coming years, indicating the significance of 360-degree customer view and experience. Rapidly changing customer needs and expectations are poised to result in tremendous pressure on organizations to prioritize customer experience for pushing brand loyalty and their customer base. Mounting competition across industries is creating the requirement for organizations to understand and gain insights into their customers across diverse touch-points to meet evolving expectations. The 360-degree customer view allows companies to maintain pace with changing customer behavior and create a one window for customer information. The approach provides companies with notable opportunities for predicting future actions of customers for personalized recommendations. In addition to understanding customer interactions across different channels like websites, online platforms and apps, the 360-degree customer view allows businesses to uncover critical insights such as what a customer purchases, how a customer shops, and preferences. The holistic view of customer data is poised to go a long way in enabling organizations to deal with unpredictable behavior and conditions while delivering seamless, tailored customer experience.


The 360-degree customer experience trend is making a big splash in the business domain and has emerged as one of the key metrics that determine future success of an organization in the digital world. Increasing consumer demand for connections, convenience and compassion is driving players to compete on customer experience, with 360-degree customer experience trend paving way for notable paradigm shifts. In the recent years, a large number of organizations have started dedicating significant resources to adopt the 360-degree customer experience. There are numerous persistent and emerging customer experience trends that are poised to redefine the way business operate and serve their customers. In today’s highly competitive global business environment, it is important for companies to devise strategies for improving both customer experience (CX) and user experience (UX). While usually considered as having similar meaning, both CX and UX have entirely different definitions. For instance, CX encompasses various factors including product designing, marketing, as well as after sale service. On the other hand, UX refers to the process associated with designing easy to use and user-friendly websites as well as mobile applications. With an intention of expanding their footprints, companies are devising a wide range of novel strategies. Given below are some of the emerging trends that are fuelling growth for various global businesses.


  • Surface-Level to 360-Degree Insights into Customers - Companies are shifting their attention from surface-level to deeper insights into customers and their behavior to ensure a telescopic view for enhanced experience. Emerging technologies such as the Internet of Things are enabling businesses to get a holistic view of markets and customers. Some of the leading brands are betting on wearable technology like bands that eliminate the need for people to carry wallets or tickets while allowing companies to capture significant amount of data to gain better insights. These solutions are enabling brands to collect information such as customer preferences, purchasing behavior and likes or dislikes. The use of machine vision technology helps companies in gathering data through facial expressions while AI is being used for deriving meaningful insights from the data to understand customers in a better manner. The application of digital tools along with data presents new opportunities for companies to know customer requirements and preferences for informed decision making. Manufacturing players are embracing SaaS and digital platforms for understanding needs of their clients and gaining high visibility to adapt the production process. In the coming years, companies tapping data and associated insights are anticipated to make better decisions to keep customers happy, improve satisfaction and drive business.
  • Right Response at Right Time - The right response at the right time is a crucial aspect of the 360-degree customer experience and gaining notable attention from organizations. Deeper insights into customers go a long way in enabling companies to deliver the right content, advertising and purchasing options at the right time or specific time of the day. The trend focuses on mass customization along with micro-moments for providing customers with desired offering at the right moment to satiate the need. Various music streaming players are offering personalized playlists to customers in line with their specific needs during the particular part of the day, like during commuting and workout. In addition, brands such as Netflix are offering recommendations to viewers on the basis of their viewing patterns. In addition to tapping data and analytics tools, companies maintain a consistent attention on communicating with their customers to observe behavior and facilitate emotional connectivity to serve them better. These customized offering are directly influenced by customer data, information and related insights, enabling companies to meet customer preferences and improve the overall experience.

The approach is prompting businesses to rely on data and predictive analytics for enhanced experience. Various players offering industrial equipment are using data from systems and equipment for scheduling service visits and maintenance tasks. The approach allows businesses to identify the need for desired intervention while minimizing the risk of unexpected breakdown or downtime. The application of predictive analytics is assisting companies to improve convenience. In addition, the use of data and facial recognition in restaurants allows the staff to learn various things about customers, such as their like or dislikes and allergies for making recommendations as per their preferences. Predictive analytics is anticipated to garner more attention to help companies in predicting the demand, making effective plans and reducing waste.

The trend is gaining momentum with increasing preference of customers for self-service options from service providers and suppliers. Rising demand for self-services has resulted in extensive adoption of bots that are being used to interact with customers, chatting on different websites and answering texts. Artificial intelligence is enabling companies to assist customers with proactive help and enhanced decision making. Ongoing advancements related to AI are anticipated to add new aspects to self-service options.
